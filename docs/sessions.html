Sessions & Workshops

CAA Mobile GIS Special Interest Group sessions on mobile GIS for archaeology

The Mobile GIS SIG organizes scientific sessions at CAA conferences to facilitate knowledge exchange, hands-on training, and discussion among members. Our sessions focus on practical applications, methodological developments, and collaborative problem-solving in the field of mobile GIS for archaeology. Since 2017, we have been hosting annual sessions that bring together researchers and practitioners from around the world to share experiences and advance the field.

Session Focus Areas

Field Methodologies

Development and evaluation of field survey techniques, data collection workflows, and best practices for mobile GIS in archaeological fieldwork.

Technical Innovations

Exploration of new technologies, GNSS advancements, software developments, and integration of mobile GIS with other tools like drones and sensors.

Data Standards

Discussion of data quality, FAIR principles, interoperability, and standardization in mobile GIS data collection and management.

Collaborative Research

Team-based collaboration, community empowerment, citizen science, and the social aspects of mobile GIS in archaeology.
