Sessions & Workshops

Hands-on workshops and technical sessions on mobile GIS for archaeology

The Mobile GIS SIG organizes various sessions, workshops, and meetings to facilitate knowledge exchange, hands-on training, and discussion among members. Our sessions focus on practical applications, technical testing, and collaborative problem-solving in the field of mobile GIS for archaeology.

Upcoming Sessions

Scientific Session

Date: To be announced

Location: TBA

Practical workshop focusing on efficient workflows for archaeological data collection using mobile applications. Participants will learn best practices for field survey, data quality assurance, and integration with other tools.

Topics covered:
  • Setting up mobile GIS applications for field work
  • Data collection workflows at different scales
  • Quality control and validation techniques
  • Integration with GNSS and other equipment

Session Types

Hands-on Workshops

Practical training sessions where participants work directly with mobile GIS applications and equipment in simulated or real field conditions.

Technical Sessions

Presentations and demonstrations of new technologies, research results, and technical developments in mobile GIS for archaeology.

Discussion Forums

Collaborative meetings focused on developing standards, sharing experiences, and addressing challenges in mobile GIS implementation.

Field Testing Events

Real-world testing of mobile applications and workflows in active archaeological field projects.
