Get in touch with the Mobile GIS Special Interest Group

We encourage all interested CAA members to join the SIG. Whether you're an experienced mobile GIS user or just getting started with mobile technologies in archaeology, we welcome your participation and look forward to sharing experiences and ideas.

Join the SIG

Interested in becoming a member of the Mobile GIS SIG? We welcome archaeologists, researchers, developers, and anyone interested in mobile technologies for archaeological fieldwork.

Membership Benefits:

  • Access to hands-on workshops and training sessions
  • Participation in collaborative research projects
  • Early testing of new mobile GIS applications
  • Network with international experts in the field
  • Contribute to standards and guidelines development
  • Share field survey experiences and best practices

Get Involved

There are many ways to participate in the Mobile GIS SIG:

Attend Sessions

Join our workshops, technical sessions, and discussion forums to learn and share knowledge.

Contribute to Projects

Participate in research projects, application testing, or standards development.

Share Your Experience

Present your field survey experiences, case studies, or technical solutions.

Propose New Initiatives

Suggest new research directions, tools to test, or topics to explore.

Contact Information

For inquiries about membership, sessions, projects, or general questions about the Mobile GIS SIG, please reach out through the CAA organization or attend one of our sessions at CAA conferences.

Note: This is a Special Interest Group of Computer Applications and Quantitative Methods in Archaeology (CAA). For official contact information and membership details, please visit the CAA website or contact us through CAA channels.

Stay Connected
